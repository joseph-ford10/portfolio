Welcome to my portfolio

With a background in social science and a grounding in full-stack web development technologies, I bring critical thinking, strong communication, and attention to detail to all that I do. Having worked on a range of projects, I want to continue using my skills to find innovative solutions to complex problems.

Here are some of the projects I've worked on:

  • SubMinder

    I was a team member on this app that helps users to track their subscriptions. I largely worked on the back-end and API for the add, delete, and update functionality using Typescript, Knex, and Express. I'm very proud with the work we did on this app and pleased that I had the opportunity to practice back-end development. Going forward, I am planning to do some work on the front-end by making more responsive.

  • I've Got

    An app that recommends activities to users based on the time they have available. Prototype built using React and CSS, with back-end functionality using Knex and SQLite in progress. The idea for this app came from having been in situations where I had a small amount of time to fill with no strong idea of how to fill it. I like what I've got so far, although, again, it is a work in progress.

  • A Tale of Two Shift Keys

    A simple typing game built using vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I've always enjoyed typing games and wanted a way to practice what I had learned in the foundations stage of Dev Academy. It looks very simple to me now, although I do like the way that the text highlights as the user types.
